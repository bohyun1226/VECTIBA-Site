The problem

Cross-border car trade is slow, opaque and risky.

Three barriers keep dealers from sourcing the best cars abroad. Vectiba removes all three.

🛃

Borders & tariffs

Tariff codes and paperwork change constantly. One mistake means a container held at port and penalties.

💬

Language

Listings and documents in a foreign language are hard to trust when tens of thousands are on the line.

📦

Buying unseen

Dealers can't fly out to inspect every car. Without verified data, remote purchase is a gamble.

How Vectiba works

From listing to your lot — one platform.

The dealer stays in the office. Vectiba and its local partners do the rest, and the AI agent flags problems before they happen.

1 · Choose your inspection

You pick the checks that matter. Our agent assigns a certified inspector near the seller, so you bid on verified facts — not pretty photos.

2 · See the landed cost

Vehicle price plus shipping and customs to your destination, in one number, before you bid.

3 · Bid across borders

Bid directly on cars in other countries. Win, and the platform books export, customs and shipping.

4 · Problems caught early

Tariff-code changes, blocked shipping lanes and missing inspection items are detected and handled before they cost you.

5 · Track door to door

Follow every car from the seller's yard to your lot, like a delivery app.

6 · Safe settlement

Escrow-backed payment so you focus on buying and selling — not chasing money.
